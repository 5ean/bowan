A Simple Financial Historical chart

Linear chart is very common in any third part library. However, this demo illustrates how to develop a Google finance-like historical chart in WPF. When you move your mouse on the chart, the crosshair will trace its movements and show the values at the junction of lines and crosshair. It also demonstrates how to use adorner control to implement cursors and crosshairs on a linear chart.
